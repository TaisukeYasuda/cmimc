How to Participate

Form a Team

Form a team of 4-6 students. Make sure you have a diverse set of skills to cover the team and power round.

Register Online

Register your team by logging in. Be sure to keep track of all important deadlines and dates.

Travel to CMU

After receiving the confirmation email for your registration, book travel to Carnegie Mellon University.
