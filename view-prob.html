Topic: Combi

Problem

Calculate $\sum_{k=0}^n\binom{n}k$.

Answer

$2^n$

Solution

Consider all the subsets of $\{1,2,\dots,n\}$. There are $2^n$ of these, consisting of $\binom{n}0$ subsets of size $0$, $\binom{n}1$ subsets of size $1$, etc. so $\sum_{k=0}^n\binom{n}k=2^n$.
